Minglin Lu is the founder and principal of GameMonkey, a no-idea studio in Shenzhen and Guilin. Aside from business work, projects include everything could be negotiated with him. The two projects codenamed Elasticsearch and CockRoach DB are his main research direction.

He loves music, especially piano. He can play one piano song named to love's end, posts photos to Flickr, creates 400x300px works-in-progress on Dribbble, sometimes makes moving pictures on Vimeo, records items of interest at Svpply, listens to all his music on Rdio and answers the occasional question on Quora. He also makes music as Murders & Mysteries and released an album in 2010.

An game boy, Minglin had been to three countries. He currently lives in Shenzhen, China, a place where he feels comfortable in his own skin.
